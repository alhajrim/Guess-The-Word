How to Play

  • 1A hidden word is shown as blank tiles. Guess it before the timer runs out.
  • 2Type a single letter to guess one letter, or type the full word to guess it all at once.
  • 3Correct letters turn green. Wrong guesses are limited — Easy: 6, Medium: 5, Hard: 4.
  • 4You start with 500 points. Each wrong guess costs −40 pts. Winning gives a time bonus.
  • 5Use the 💡 Hint button to reveal a letter — costs −50 pts and −5 seconds, max 3 per round.
  • 6Pick Easy, Medium, or Hard on the main menu.
